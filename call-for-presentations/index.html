The 3rd workshop on Energy, Infrastructure and Environment Research (EIER 2022), hosted by VNU University of Engineering and Technology (VNU-UET) will be taking place on January 13, 2022. Due to the current state of the pandemic in Vietnam, this event will be held fully online.

The objectives of the EIER workshop series are:
  • to raise awareness of, and to nourish high quality research in problems of direct relevance to energy, infrastructure and environment;
  • to foster collaboration between national and international scholars; and
  • to further research engagement with responsible stakeholders towards feasible solutions to the burning issues in energy, infrastructure, and environment.

For more details on the workshop program and schedule, visit the EIER 2022 website at https://avitech.uet.vnu.edu.vn/en/the-3rd-workshop-on-energy-infrastructure-and-environment-research-eier-2022-2/.

We welcome submissions for short presentations on topics relevant to the objectives of the workshop. Please send an abstract of your presentation proposal to tranhiep.dinh@vnu.edu.vn. Presenters will also be asked to submit a pre-recorded video of their presentation (in English). Length of the video must not exceed five minutes. Short presentations should focus on ideas, applications, and their impact rather than on technical details.

The deadline for abstract submissions is December 31, 2021, and the deadline for submissions of video presentations is January 06, 2022.

Please visit EIER 2022 website for submission instructions.
